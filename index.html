// WHY I BUILT BHARAT MONITOR
01
India's governance data is scattered across 50+ portals — I wanted a single DEFCON-style interface that unifies fiscal, defence, peace, and project intelligence in real time.
02
Most dashboards show stale numbers; I built this with a live AI backbone so every metric you see is freshly reasoned from the latest available data, not hardcoded six months ago.
03
As an Indian builder, I believe citizens deserve a military-grade situational awareness layer for their own country — transparent, open, and beautiful.
04
Inspired by worldmonitor.app, I wanted to prove the same intelligence-dashboard concept could be built specifically for India's complex federal structure and 28-state economy.
05
This is open civic tech — a statement that powerful geopolitical tools shouldn't be locked behind enterprise paywalls. Bharat Monitor is free, forever.
